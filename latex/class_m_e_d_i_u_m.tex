\doxysubsection{Detailed Description}
A derived class representing the \doxylink{class_m_e_d_i_u_m}{MEDIUM} difficulty settings. 

This class sets the parameters for the \doxylink{class_m_e_d_i_u_m}{MEDIUM} difficulty level, with a balanced distribution of platform types, and a moderate chance for bonus items to appear. The platform distance is also in a middle range.
